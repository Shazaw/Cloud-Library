CRIME AND PUNISHMENT
By Fyodor Dostoevsky

A psychological thriller that follows the mental anguish and moral dilemmas of Rodion Raskolnikov, an impoverished ex-student who formulates and executes a plan to kill a pawnbroker for her money.
